## 6. Success Metrics (90‑day Targets)

| Metric | Target (by D+90) | Measurement Tool |
|--------|------------------|------------------|
| **Daily Active Users (DAU)** | **≥ 500** unique users (ops staff & analysts) | Mixpanel / internal telemetry |
| **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)** | **$150,000** (≈ $1.8 M ARR) | Stripe / Chargebee |
| **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** | ≤ **$3,200** avg | Finance dashboard |
| **Lead‑to‑Customer Conversion** | ≥ **12%** (MQL → Closed‑Won) | CRM (HubSpot) |
| **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** | ≥ **55** (post‑implementation survey) | SurveyMonkey |
| **Time‑to‑Resolution (TTR) Reduction** | ≥ **60%** vs baseline (pilot) | Platform analytics |

*If any metric falls >15% short of target, trigger a 2‑week sprint to iterate messaging, pricing, or onboarding flow.*
